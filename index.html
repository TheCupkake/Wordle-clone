How To Play

Guess the player name in 6 tries. After each guess the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the solution.

Rules

  • Each guess should be a valid football player's name, past or present.
  • Only football players in the game are the ones whose names contain 6 letters.
  • If you're stuck, you can use a single hint.
  • If you use a hint, you will have 1 less try to guess the player.
  • Our database doesn't have all the 6-letter players who have ever played in it, so don't get mad if a guess gets rejected :)
  • Accents, apostrophes, and other special characters are ignored, for example, Mané Would be:
    • M
    A
    N
    E
  • Names that include a prefix or separate passages are considered a single name, for example:
    • D
    E
    G
    E
    A

    Is a valid guess (ignoring the fact that he has only 5 letters in his name).

About

Recently I have been obsessed with the Wordle game and couldn't stop playing it or thinking about. So much so that I have listened to Josh Wardle for over an hour talking about it on the Syntax podcast. And I was very impressed by his entire story and why he created it in the first place and how it got viral. I was also fascinated by his enthusiasm in telling the story. I always thought he was this accomplished developer but I was struck by his simplicity and humble ingenuity.

So, as a die hard football fan, I thought there must be an equivalent for us football nerds. And being a fan of Sky Sports games like who am I, I decided to incorporate those two together instead of just plainly copying Wordle in the hope of people enjoying it and having fun. I have been playing this game religiously myself!
